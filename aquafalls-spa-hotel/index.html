The Hotel

A Five-Star Retreat Surrounded by Ancient Forest

Aquafalls Spa Hotel sits within Peneda-Gerês National Park — Portugal's only national park and one of the last truly wild landscapes in Western Europe. Cascading rivers, granite peaks, and ancient oak forests form the backdrop for an experience that is both deeply restorative and unmistakably luxurious.

Recently renovated and expanded, the hotel combines contemporary spa design with the warmth of traditional Minho hospitality. Our team goes out of their way to ensure every stay is memorable — from suite upgrades to personalised dining recommendations in the Minho region.

Whether you are seeking a wellness retreat, a romantic escape, or a base from which to explore the National Park, Aquafalls offers everything you need under one roof.
